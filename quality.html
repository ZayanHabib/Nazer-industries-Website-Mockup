Quality Control

Zero-Compromise Precision
Our Standard

Excellence in Every Piece

For Nazer Industries, quality control is a critical component in the manufacture of brass fittings, ensuring each piece meets the dimensional accuracy, durability, and performance standards required across various applications.

The process begins with verifying the Brass composition, as even small deviations in copper–zinc ratios can affect strength and corrosion resistance. Once machining begins, dimensional inspection becomes essential.

The Process

Systematic Inspection

Dimensional Accuracy

We rely on precision tools such as digital calipers and thread gauges to measure internal and external diameters, thread pitch, and depth with high accuracy.

Go/No-Go Evaluations

Fittings undergo rigorous "go/no-go" evaluations to ensure they fall within tight tolerances and meet strict engineering standards.

Mechanical Testing

Systematic and thorough measurement, surface-finish checks, and mechanical testing ensure every fitting performs reliably in demanding environments.

Precision Instruments

Calibrated for Perfection

Our quality control team utilizes the latest in precision measurement technology. From digital height gauges to specialized thread micrometers, every instrument is regularly calibrated to maintain the highest levels of accuracy.
